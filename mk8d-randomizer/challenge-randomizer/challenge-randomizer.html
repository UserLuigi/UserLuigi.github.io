Challenge Randomizer

Welcome to UserLuigi's Challenge Randomizer for Mario Kart 8 Deluxe!
This is an easy to use and simple challenge randomizer for the latest Mario Kart entry, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe. It will randomly select a challenge that you will have to follow during an online race.

Just click the button below to get a brand new challenge. Have fun!

If you fail to do your challenge, or not get first place, you have to redo the challenge.
